New features:

  • Disk Texture now can open and view .blp and .dds files. You don't need to save png files again!
  • Disk Texture now saves the path to the texture in your PC. You don't need to re-apply textures after loading anymore! (Unless you change the place of the texture)
  • Export now copies the code to clipboard, meaning you can press CTRL+V to paste immediately!
